# No-Leaf-Clover
The "No Leaf Clover" Story - Tales of Luck and Life: 

Surviving the Storm

My name is Tommy Sr. For a long time, I lived in the shadow of "should." I believed I should have been stronger, should have seen the signs, and should have been able to protect everyone I love from the storms of life.
But life doesn't always give you a four-leaf clover. Sometimes, it gives you the "No Leaf Clover"—the moment when the luck runs out and you’re standing face-to-face with the unthinkable.

I have walked through the fire:

I am a survivor of childhood abuse that I carried in silence for decades.
I have battled the dark road of addiction to prescription medication and alcohol.
I have felt the shattering weight of losing my oldest son, Tommy Jr., to an overdose.
I have watched a lifetime of financial security vanish into a fraudulent Ponzi scheme.

Why am I telling you this?

Because for years, I felt like the reason my family "fell apart" was me. I felt like I was the only one who had failed this badly. I was wrong. I’m starting this site because I’ve learned that the fight never ends, but you don't have to fight it alone. My door is open. Whether you are dealing with the echoes of trauma, the heavy fog of grief, or the struggle to rebuild after losing everything—I am here.

I am not a victim of my past; I am a witness to the fact that you can lose everything and still stand "Unbowed."

Welcome to No Leaf Clover. Let’s keep moving forward.
